Pod System Rules

Board-Wipe operates on a friendly but structured pod system. Here's what you need to know:

Core Rule #1

Respect & Inclusivity: All players are welcome regardless of skill level. We maintain a welcoming environment for everyone from beginners to competitive players.

Core Rule #2

Fair Play: No cheating, collusion, or unfair advantage. We follow official MTG rules of conduct. Violations result in warnings and potential removal.

Core Rule #3

Pod Rotation: Pods rotate regularly to ensure everyone plays with different opponents and experiences variety in the meta.

Core Rule #4

Time Management: Games have time limits. Casual play: 50 minutes. Competitive: 60 minutes. Please be punctual and play at a reasonable pace.

Supported Formats

We support various Magic formats to keep gameplay fresh and engaging:

Standard

Using only recently released sets. Perfect for staying current with the meta and official tournament formats.

Commander / EDH

Our most popular casual format! 100-card singleton decks. Multiplayer pod play (3-4 players per pod).

Limited

Draft and Sealed events. Build decks from limited card pools. Great for new players and learning!

Modern & Pioneer

Constructed formats with larger card pools. Available by request for dedicated competitive players.

How Our Pods Work

Pod Formation

Players are grouped into pods of 3-4 players. Each pod plays independently, and results are tracked for ranking and seasonal rewards.

Scoring System

Win-Based: Traditional win/loss records are kept. Points: Wins = 3 points, Draws = 1 point, Losses = 0 points. Players with the most points rank highest.

Seasonal Rewards

Top performers in each season receive recognition and potential prizes. All contributors earn store credit or promo cards at the end of each season.

Player Conduct & Etiquette

DO:

  • Be respectful to all players
  • Play at a reasonable pace
  • Communicate clearly about game state
  • Help newer players learn
  • Report issues to organizers
  • Have fun!

DON'T:

  • Cheat or bend rules
  • Be disruptive or aggressive
  • Angle shoot or mislead
  • Use banned cards
  • Ignore organizer rulings
  • Play while intoxicated

Questions About Our Rules?

If you have any questions about our pod system, formats, or specific rulings, don't hesitate to reach out. Our organizers are here to help!
