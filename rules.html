Pod System Rules

Board-Wipe operates on a friendly but structured pod system. Here's what you need to know:

Rule 1

Infinite Combos: Infinite combos may be executed no more than twice per turn.

Rule 2

Gamechanger Limit: Only one "Gamechanger" card is allowed per deck.

Rule 3

Land Destruction: Land destruction is permitted and may target utility lands (e.g., Rogue's Passage, Reliquary Tower).

Rule 4

Decks Allowed: All deck archetypes are allowed, regardless of their popularity.

Supported Formats

We currently run two formats at Board-Wipe:

Commander / EDH

Our primary format: 100-card singleton multiplayer pods (typically 3–4 players). Casual-focused, social multiplayer games.

Limited (Draft & Sealed)

Draft and Sealed events where decks are built from limited card pools — great for events, rotating experiences, and new players.

How Our Pods Work

Casual Meetups

Board-Wipe runs simple, casual meetups — no competitive seasons, rankings, or seasonal rewards. Our events are focused on fun and community.

Pod Formation

Players are grouped into pods of 3–4 players. Pods play independently — casual pairings designed for relaxed play and variety.

Community Events

We also organise non-card community events such as movie nights and out-of-card activities (e.g., darts). These are optional and aimed at socialising.

FAQ — Formats & Events

Which formats do you run?

We currently run Commander/EDH and Limited (Draft & Sealed).

Event cadence

EDH meetups are held weekly or biweekly depending on attendance. Prerelease draft events take place around new set releases — check our announcements for exact dates.

Player Conduct & Etiquette

DO:

  • Be respectful to all players
  • Play at a reasonable pace
  • Communicate clearly about game state
  • Help newer players learn
  • Report issues to organizers
  • Have fun!

DON'T:

  • Cheat or bend rules
  • Be disruptive or aggressive
  • Angle shoot or mislead
  • Use banned cards
  • Ignore organizer rulings
  • Play while intoxicated
